## Core Principles

- Follow the documentation before making implementation decisions.
- Never invent UI components that are not specified.
- Never change design tokens without explicit approval.
- Prioritize accessibility, performance, maintainability, and SEO.
- Prefer composition over duplication.
- Keep commits focused on a single concern.

## Out of Scope

Do not introduce additional frameworks, state management libraries, or backend services unless explicitly requested.
